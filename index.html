Company Overview

Established in 2021, PT Mitra Kawan Bersama (MKB) operates as a logistics and workforce solutions provider focused on the oil and gas sector. The company integrates operational execution with structured project coordination to support large-scale industrial activities.

With an investment exceeding 2 trillion IDR and nationwide operational coverage, MKB ensures consistent service delivery across diverse project environments, supported by CCPM-integrated planning and reporting systems.
