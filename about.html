About PT Mitra Kawan Bersama

Established in 2021, PT Mitra Kawan Bersama (MKB) is a strategic logistics and workforce solutions provider with a proven record of supporting Indonesia’s upstream oil and gas sector. The company operates with strong financial backing, proprietary equipment assets, and extensive industry expertise.

MKB delivers integrated solutions across the full project lifecycle, ranging from mobilization and site setup to ongoing field operations and performance monitoring. The company’s operational model is structured to ensure efficiency, transparency, and measurable project outcomes.

Supported by CCPM-integrated systems, MKB ensures real-time coordination, compliance monitoring, and data-driven decision-making across all project environments.

  • Personnel: 450+ Field Workforce

Mission

To deliver tailored, performance-driven solutions that empower clients to achieve operational objectives with efficiency, safety, and transparency.

Investment & Operational Scale

Since its inception, MKB has invested over IDR 2 trillion in operational infrastructure, enabling full ownership and control of its fleet and equipment. This ensures operational reliability, rapid mobilization, and cost efficiency.

The company maintains a fleet of over 60 heavy equipment units and more than 30 light vehicles, supporting industrial-scale operations across multiple regions in Indonesia.

All assets are certified and integrated within CCPM systems, ensuring compliance, traceability, and operational readiness.
